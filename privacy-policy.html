Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Brainservice Incorporated (“we”, “us”, or “our”) develops mobile applications and digital services (“Services”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our Services.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications and services published by Brainservice Incorporated, including general-audience apps and child-directed (kids) apps.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

Some Services may allow users to create accounts, sign in using third-party authentication providers (such as Google or Apple), or contact us. In such cases, we may collect information such as:

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Authentication identifiers provided by third-party sign-in services

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

When using our Services, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage and interaction data
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (for non-child-directed apps only)

4. Child-Directed (Kids) Applications

Some of our Services may be directed toward children under the age of 13. For these child-directed apps:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
  • We do not require account creation for children unless explicitly stated.
  • We do not use personalized advertising.
  • Any analytics or ads used are configured to comply with applicable child-privacy laws, including COPPA and Google Play Families policies.

If we ever collect personal information from children, it will be done only with verifiable parental consent and clearly disclosed within the app.

5. How We Use Information

Information collected may be used to:

  • Provide and maintain the Services
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Analyze app usage and performance
  • Serve advertisements (non-child-directed apps only)
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

6. Third-Party Services

Our Services may use third-party services such as analytics providers, advertising networks, authentication services, and cloud infrastructure (for example: Google Firebase, Google AdMob, Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In).

These third-party services may collect information in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage users and parents to review those policies.

7. Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by law.

8. User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Requests can be made by contacting us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
