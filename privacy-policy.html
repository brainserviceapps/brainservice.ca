Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Brainservice Incorporated (“we”, “us”, or “our”) develops mobile applications and digital services (“Services”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our Services.

2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications and services published by Brainservice Incorporated, including general-audience apps and child-directed (kids) apps.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

Some Services may allow account creation, third-party sign-in (such as Google or Apple), or user communication. Information collected may include:

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • Authentication identifiers provided by sign-in providers

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

When using our Services, certain information may be collected automatically:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage and interaction data
  • Crash reports and performance diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (non-child-directed apps only)

4. Child-Directed (Kids) Applications

Some Services may be intended for children under the age of 13. For these apps:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
  • We do not require account creation for children unless explicitly stated
  • We do not use personalized advertising
  • Any analytics or ads comply with COPPA and Google Play Families policies

If personal information is ever collected from a child, it will only occur with verifiable parental consent and clear disclosure.

5. How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain Services
  • Improve functionality and user experience
  • Analyze performance and usage trends
  • Serve ads in non-child-directed apps
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal requirements

6. Third-Party Services

Our Services may integrate third-party services such as analytics providers, advertising networks, authentication services, and cloud infrastructure (e.g., Firebase, Google AdMob, Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In).

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

7. Data Security and Retention

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect data. Information is retained only as long as necessary or legally required.

8. User Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by contacting us.

9. Policy Updates

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
