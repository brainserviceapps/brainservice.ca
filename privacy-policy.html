Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Identity of Data Controller

Brainservice Incorporated ("Brainservice", "we", "us", or "our") is a corporation organized under the laws of Ontario, Canada. For purposes of global data protection laws, Brainservice acts as the data controller for personal information collected through its Services.

2. Scope of Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all mobile applications, games, educational tools, and digital services published by Brainservice Incorporated worldwide, including general-audience and child-directed applications.

3. Categories of Information Collected

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Name or display name
  • Email address
  • OAuth authentication identifiers (Google, Apple, etc.)
  • Communications and support inquiries

3.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • IP address and approximate region
  • Usage analytics and event interactions
  • Crash reports and diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (non-child-directed apps only)

3.3 Payment Information

All subscription and purchase transactions are processed exclusively by Apple App Store or Google Play. Brainservice does not collect or store payment card information.

4. Legal Bases for Processing (GDPR Article 6)

  • User consent
  • Performance of a contract
  • Legitimate business interests (analytics, fraud prevention, product improvement)
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. Purpose Limitation & Data Minimization

We collect only information reasonably necessary to operate, secure, and improve our Services. We do not process personal information for purposes materially unrelated to those described in this Policy.

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA & Global Child Laws)

Certain Services may be directed to children under 13 years of age. For child-directed apps:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information without verifiable parental consent.
  • Personalized advertising is disabled.
  • Analytics tools are configured in child-compliant mode.
  • No behavioral profiling is conducted.

If we become aware of unauthorized collection of a child's personal information, we will delete it promptly. Parents may contact us to review or delete a child's data.

7. Advertising & Analytics Disclosure

We use third-party services including Firebase, Google Analytics, and Google Ads (AdMob). These providers may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage data under their own privacy policies.

Users may limit advertising tracking via device-level privacy settings. California residents may opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information where applicable.

8. CCPA / CPRA (California Residents)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to request deletion
  • Right to correct inaccurate information
  • Right to opt-out of sale or sharing
  • Right to non-discrimination

We do not sell personal information for monetary compensation.

9. International Data Transfers

Personal information may be processed in Canada, the United States, or other jurisdictions. By using our Services, you consent to international transfers of data. Where required, we rely on contractual safeguards and standard data protection clauses.

10. Data Retention

  • Account data retained until user deletion request
  • Analytics retained per provider configuration
  • Data may be retained for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution

11. Security Measures

We implement administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards including encryption in transit, access controls, and limited access to personal information. However, no system is guaranteed secure.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a legally reportable data breach, we will notify affected users and regulatory authorities as required by applicable law.

13. Automated Decision-Making

We do not engage in automated decision-making that produces legal or similarly significant effects on users.
